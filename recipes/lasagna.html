The Lord's Lasagna

Description

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with tomato sauce and a delicious, cheesy filling.

I have not found a better lasagna anywhere. Serve it with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread, trust me!

This is the greatest lasagna you will ever eat. Its heavenly texture is a true blessing from above. Consume at your own risk.

You will never be the same!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef

  • 1 can crushed tomatoes

  • 1 egg

  • 2 cans tomato paste

  • 1 half cup water

  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

  • 12 lasagna noodles

  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese

  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced

  • 3/4 cup grated parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the shit a good amount and simmer that bitch

  2. Boil some water and throw them noodles in it until they're done

  3. Mix the egg with the ricotta cheese

  4. Put the oven at 375 degrees and wait till she's ready to cook

  5. Throw everything into the oven in a pile and take it out when it looks good

  6. Eat it but make sure it's not too hot
